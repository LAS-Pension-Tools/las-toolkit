Notes

  • Officers/practice staff: Tiering uses last year’s WTE pensionable pay unless there is a change in circumstances (new starter, promotion/step down, rate change expected to last ≥12 months), in which case use this year’s estimated annualised pay.
  • Part‑time officers: Use whole‑time equivalent pay to set the tier. Unsocial hours are added after the WTE basic is calculated.
  • Practitioners/non‑GP providers: Your tier is based on total pensionable practitioner income in the year.
  • 2022/23 split: From 1 Oct 2022 a new structure of bands/rates applies. Choose “2022/23 (Apr–Sep)” for the first half of the year and “2022/23 (Oct–Mar)” for the second half.
